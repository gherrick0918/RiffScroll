## Color Legend

Throughout the app, these colors are used consistently:

- **Primary Blue**: Selected items, important actions
- **Accent Gold**: Today, level indicators, special highlights
- **Success Green**: Completed items, positive actions
- **Warning Yellow**: Scheduled/pending items
- **Danger Red**: Delete actions, warnings
- **Info Cyan**: Informational badges
- **Secondary Gray**: Less important items, disabled states
